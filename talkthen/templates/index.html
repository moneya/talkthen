How it works

FAQ




In the beginning

When you schedule a call, the organizer will be texted to confirm the call. Cancel at any time by replying to the text.


At call time

The organizer gets a call from a 415 number. He or she is connected to the other person's line. When the other person picks up, it just becomes a normal phone call.

In the event that the other person doesn't pick up, treat it like a normal call - leave a message or hang up.


Pricing
