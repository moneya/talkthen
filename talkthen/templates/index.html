FAQ




How it works

When you schedule a call, both parties will receive a text confirming the date & time of the phone call. Cancel at any time by replying to the text.

Or, if you provide an email after signup, you'll get an email confirmation instead. Some people prefer this so they can forward the email themselves.


When the call happens

You'll get a call from a 415 number. The first person to pick up will hear the phone ringing. When the other person picks up, it just becomes a normal phone call.

In the event that the other person doesn't pick up, you can treat it like a normal call - leave a message or hang up.


Pricing

This is currently a free service. Eventually we'll charge for things like large conference calls or automatic call recording.
